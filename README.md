# linear-regression – Ecommerce Clients

**Project Overview**

➡️ This project uses Linear Regression to analyze customer behavior in an e-commerce platform and determine whether the company should focus more on its mobile app or website to increase customer spending.

**Dataset Features**

✔️ Avg. Session Length

✔️ Time on App

✔️ Time on Website

✔️ Length of Membership

**Objective**

➡️ To identify the key factors that influence customer spending and support data-driven business decisions.

**Key Insights**

✔️ Length of Membership is the most significant factor affecting customer spending.

✔️ Time on App has a strong positive impact on spending.

✔️ Time on Website shows very little correlation with spending.

**Conclusion**

➡️ The results suggest that customers are more influenced by the mobile app than the website.
    Therefore, the company should consider investing more in improving the mobile app experience while also evaluating potential improvements to the website.

**Tools Used**

➡️ Python, Jupyter Notebook, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Scikit-learn.
